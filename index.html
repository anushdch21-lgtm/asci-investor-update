Our Evolution: From Farms to Finance to Flood Intelligence
We began by addressing climate risk for smallholder farmers, using satellite data to make climate losses measurable at the farm level. This capability naturally extended into insurance, where the same data became relevant for underwriting, claims assessment, and parametric risk design. Early insurer pilots and validations helped us understand what it takes to move from analytics to insurance-grade risk intelligence, even as full productization remained constrained by capital.

Today, ASCI has evolved into flood intelligence. Floods‚Äîone of the most destructive and cross-cutting climate perils‚Äînow sit at the core of our platform, enabling decision-making across agriculture, energy, infrastructure, and urban assets.
‚ñ∏
Why Flood is Peril #1
$50B+ annual global losses. Most under-insured peril. Unlike wildfire or earthquake, flood risk changes rapidly with urbanization. Our CFD simulations + real-time satellite monitoring quantify this dynamic risk at asset level.
‚ñ∏
Risk-Resilience Framework
Climate Risk & Resilience Index translates physical hazards into financial metrics: Mean Damage Ratios, Climate VaR, Retrofit CapEx. TCFD-aligned, IFRS S2 compliant. Integrates into DCF models for climate-adjusted valuations.
